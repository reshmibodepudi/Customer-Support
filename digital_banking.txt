DBS Digital Banking Services

1. NetBanking:
   - 24/7 access to account balance, statements, fund transfers
   - NEFT, IMPS, RTGS supported
   - Secure login with OTP

2. Mobile Banking (DBS digibank app):
   - Instant account opening with Aadhaar OTP
   - Bill payments, recharges, fund transfers
   - Biometric login available

3. Security:
   - Two-factor authentication mandatory
   - Real-time fraud monitoring
