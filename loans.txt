DBS Loan Services

1. Types of loans offered:
   - Personal Loan
   - Home Loan
   - Education Loan
   - Vehicle Loan

2. Loan eligibility:
   - Age between 21 and 60 years
   - Stable source of income
   - Minimum salary: INR 25,000 per month (for salaried)
   - CIBIL score above 700

3. Documentation required:
   - Identity proof
   - Address proof
   - Salary slips / IT returns
   - Bank statements (last 6 months)

4. Loan processing time:
   - Personal Loans: 24–48 hours
   - Home Loans: 5–7 business days
