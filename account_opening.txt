DBS Account Opening Information

1. To open a new DBS savings account, customers must:
   - Provide valid government-issued ID (Aadhar, Passport, Voter ID).
   - Provide proof of address (Utility bill, Rental agreement, Driving license).
   - Submit recent passport-size photograph.
   - Complete in-person verification or eKYC.

2. Minimum balance requirements:
   - Regular Savings Account: INR 10,000
   - Digital Savings Account: INR 0 (no minimum balance)

3. Account activation timeline:
   - In-person branch applications: 2-3 business days
   - Digital applications via DBS app: Instant or within 24 hours
